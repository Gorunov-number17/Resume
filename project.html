Fixmeet.ru

Each of us at least once in life faced a problem when friends or acquaintances for various reasons could not make the company on a trip, did not want to go to some action, for example, to cinema, to theater, to club, to rollerblade or a Bicycle. To help people in this situation, we have created FIXMEET-a site where you can find new friends with common interests, ready to meet in reality, spend time together fun and active. We have gathered on our site a number of different interests: sports, movie genres, music, sport and cultural activities, types of travel etc to FIXMEET could solve all problems by finding partners and friends for meetings and joint activities. Everyone can adjust their preferences, find a suitable meeting or offer their own

For whom fixmeet.ru suetable

  • No company for an event, a movie, a trip? Friends - girlfriend refused to go? Look for the company or passengers for FIXMEET!
  • Have a favorite sport, but no partner? Do you want to assemble a team for regular games? You on FIXMEET!
  • Need to find like-minded people for joint training, development, creativity? Meet at FIXMEET!
  • Moved(and) in a new city? The old company fell apart? Or just not against new acquaintances with interesting people? Join to FIXMEET
